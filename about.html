About Our Company

LITTLEMAKER (CAMBODIA) CO., LTD. is a manufacturing company established in Cambodia, specializing in metal components, furniture hardware, and related products. The company operates manufacturing, quality control, warehousing, and supporting functions in accordance with customer, statutory, and regulatory requirements.

Our Commitment & Quality

The company is committed to providing stable quality products, on-time delivery, and continual improvement through the effective implementation of a Quality Management System (QMS) that complies with ISO 9001:2015. We are committed to continuously improving our quality management system.
